The need

Less than 1% of 38,400 deaf children in Tanzania go to school.

There are few fluent sign language teachers or interpreters in Tanzania.

Parents report their deaf children are often turned away from schools that have deaf units because they are full and not accepting new students.

The majority of deaf children reside in remote rural villages. Since there are few schools qualified to serve deaf students, this creates a logistical problem for many rural families who cannot afford to have a child travel far distance.

Deaf children are often kept at home, because it is too dangerous for the child to walk or take the limited transportation available to get to a school with a deaf unit. This inaccessibility contributes to parents keeping their deaf children out of school.

Our Solution

Our goal is to use Digital Solution to reach more families and teachers so as:

To empower families to use their communication skills

Ensure deaf childrens are included in their family life and in their community

Empower deaf children to express their own views and values and develop the social skills they need to lead independent lives

To enable deaf children with skills that can help them to fight violence against them

Allow deaf children to fulfil their potential
