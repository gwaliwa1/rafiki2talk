About US

We are a group of Tanzania youths who use digital solutions to solve community challenges

Our primary focus is on vulnerable groups: disability community, children and girls. We are group of Engineers, marketers, community experts. We use our skills to provive community challenges, with solutions that are user-friendly, while can reach the large community at reasobable and affordable resources

We live in Digital era where by information is spread quickly by digital means thus, digital girls uses digital solution to reach our targets

Our motto in this project is to "leave no deaf child out of school" By educating teachers, parents and community about sign language, as that the only way they can communicate with deaf children....Teach them them how to express themselves thus fight against abuse and discrimination the deaf community have been facing for years. Our goal is to have every deaf child in school at her/his location for their safety and convinience.

We focus our lessons on Swahili, because Swahili is native language in Tanzania. Its the media of communication of all places in Tanzania, reardless financial, religious,social difference.
